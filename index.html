Services

What we build for clients

Three things we do well. We don't take projects outside these.

AI Receptionists & Lead Systems

For businesses that lose leads after hours or waste time on manual qualification.

  • Multi-channel: WhatsApp, voice, web chat
  • Intelligent lead qualification with custom question flows
  • CRM handoff and booking integration
  • Live dashboard and conversation logs
  • 24/7 uptime with automatic failover
  • Multilingual support — Arabic, Urdu, English

Typical clients: real estate agencies (Gulf + US), any service business with inbound lead volume.

RAG Document Intelligence

For teams that spend hours searching documents that should take seconds.

  • Document ingestion pipeline (PDF, Word, web)
  • Vector indexing via Qdrant
  • Semantic search with cited, verifiable answers
  • Multi-tenant access control by firm or department
  • Admin panel for document management

Typical clients: accounting firms, legal practices, operations-heavy businesses.

Automation & Integration Pipelines

For operations still running on manual steps that could — and should — run themselves.

  • n8n or custom pipeline builds
  • API integrations across your existing stack
  • Cold outreach automation
  • Data extraction at scale
  • Scheduled jobs and webhook chains

Typical clients: agencies, SaaS companies, any team with repetitive data workflows.
